Conditions For Jimmy Gibbs Jr. To Spawn

Must be on Dead Center Atrium

Must be in Campaign (or a mutation that uses Campaign as a base)

After the finale is started, a time between 1-4 minutes later is randomly chosen

When the time is reached, Jimmy Gibbs Jr. has a 33% chance to spawn

Jimmy Gibbs Jr. can only attempt to spawn once unless the campaign is restarted

The spawn time for Jimmy Gibbs Jr. will overflow to the next round if the survivors are wiped before the time is reached
